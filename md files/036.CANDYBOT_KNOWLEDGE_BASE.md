## ?? BOOKING A DJ SLOT

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Navigate to "Book New Slot"
- Click the **"? Book New Slot"** tab at the top
- You'll see a 5-step wizard to guide you

#### Step 2: Select a Venue
- Choose from available venues in the dropdown
- Each venue has different time slots available
- **Tip**: Popular venues book up fast!

#### Step 3: Pick Your Date & Time
- Select a date from the calendar
- Choose your preferred time slot
- **Best Times**: 
  - Friday/Saturday nights (prime time!)
  - 8PM-2AM (peak audience hours)

#### Step 4: Enter DJ Information
- **DJ Name**: Your stage name (auto-filled from profile)
- **Streaming Link**: Your live stream URL
  - Format: `https://your-stream.link`
  - **Required** for online sets
  - Can use Twitch, YouTube Live, Mixcloud, etc.

#### Step 5: Upload Logo (Optional)
- Upload your DJ logo to PostImages.org
- Use **600x600 pixels** for best display
- Paste the direct link URL

#### Step 6: Confirm Booking
- Review all information
- Click **"?? BOOK SLOT ??"**
- You'll receive confirmation instantly!

**Q: Can I book multiple slots?**

**Q: Can I edit my booking?**
A: Yes! Go to "View Bookings" ? Select your booking ? Click "Edit"

**Q: What if a time slot is taken?**

## ? FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is this free to use?**
A: Yes! The DJ Booking System is completely free.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Currently desktop only, but mobile web version coming soon!

**Q: How do I get help?**
A: Just click on me (??) anytime! I'm always here to help!

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: Usually up to 3 months in advance, depending on the venue.

**Q: Can I book recurring weekly slots?**
A: Yes! Contact venue owners for regular slot arrangements.

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**
A: No problem! Just delete your booking. Try to give at least 24 hours notice.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What streaming platforms are supported?**
A: Any platform! Twitch, YouTube, Mixcloud, SoundCloud, etc.

**Q: Do I need special equipment?**
A: For online sets, you need:
  - DJ equipment (controller, mixer, etc.)
  - Computer with streaming software (OBS, etc.)
  - Stable internet connection
  - Streaming account

**Q: Can I stream my set?**
A: Yes! That's what the "Streaming Link" field is for!

## ?? TROUBLESHOOTING

### Common Issues:

#### Can't Login?
- Check username/password
- Verify internet connection
- Clear browser cache (if web version)
- Contact support

#### Can't Book a Slot?
- Verify all fields are filled
- Check if slot is still available
- Ensure date is in the future
- Try a different venue

#### Stream Link Not Working?
- Verify URL format
- Test link in browser first
- Make sure stream is live
- Use direct stream URL

#### Chat Not Loading?
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the app
- Clear chat cache
- Report to admin if persists

### Getting Support:

1. **Ask Candy-Bot** (me!) - I'm usually the fastest!
2. **Check Discord** - Community can help
3. **Contact Admin** - For account issues
4. **Email Support** - For serious problems
