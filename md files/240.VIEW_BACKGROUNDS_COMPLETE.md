**ChatView.xaml** (Line 29-31):
```xaml
<!-- Even darker -->
<ImageBrush ImageSource="/New-BG.png" Stretch="UniformToFill" Opacity="0.4"/>

<!-- Current (darker than others) -->
<ImageBrush ImageSource="/New-BG.png" Stretch="UniformToFill" Opacity="0.6"/>

<!-- Same as other views -->
<ImageBrush ImageSource="/New-BG.png" Stretch="UniformToFill" Opacity="0.8"/>
```

### Changing Stretch Mode

Available options:
- `UniformToFill` - Fill area, maintain ratio, may crop (current)
- `Uniform` - Fit in area, maintain ratio, may show borders
- `Fill` - Stretch to fill, may distort
- `None` - Original size, may tile

Example:
```xaml
<ImageBrush ImageSource="/New-BG.png" Stretch="Uniform" Opacity="0.8"/>
```

## ?? Bug Fixes

### ChatView.xaml - SpaceButton Style
**Issue**: Invalid `TargetName` attribute in style triggers  
**Error**: `MC4011: TargetName property cannot be set on a Style Setter`

**Fixed** (Lines 19-20, 23-24):
```xaml
<!-- BEFORE (Invalid) -->
<Setter Property="Background" Value="#00FF00" TargetName="border"/>

<!-- AFTER (Fixed) -->
<Setter Property="Background" Value="#00FF00"/>
```
