## ?? Configuration Options

### Adjusting Opacity
To make the background more or less visible, modify the Opacity value:
- **0.5** = 50% (more subtle)
- **0.8** = 80% (current setting - balanced)
- **1.0** = 100% (full opacity)

### Changing Stretch Mode
Available stretch options:
- `UniformToFill` - Scales to fill, may crop (current)
- `Uniform` - Scales to fit, maintains aspect ratio
- `Fill` - Stretches to fill, may distort
- `None` - Original size, may repeat

### Example Custom Settings
```xaml
<!-- More visible background -->
<ImageBrush ImageSource="New-BG.png" Stretch="UniformToFill" Opacity="1.0"/>

<!-- Centered, non-stretched -->
<ImageBrush ImageSource="New-BG.png" Stretch="None" Opacity="0.7"/>

<!-- Fit to window -->
<ImageBrush ImageSource="New-BG.png" Stretch="Uniform" Opacity="0.9"/>
```
