### Bobbing Animation:
- **Duration**: 2 seconds
- **Range**: 0 to -12 pixels (up)
- **Easing**: Sine In/Out
- **Loop**: Forever, auto-reverse

### Speech Bubble Animation:
- **Fade In**: 0 ? 1 opacity (0.3s)
- **Scale**: 0.5 ? 1.0 (0.3s)
- **Easing**: Back ease (bounce effect)
- **Auto-Hide**: After 5 seconds
