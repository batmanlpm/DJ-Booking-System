## ?? **HEADER STYLE DETAILS**

```xaml
<DataGrid.ColumnHeaderStyle>
    <Style TargetType="DataGridColumnHeader">
        <Setter Property="Background" Value="#000000"/>           <!-- Black -->
        <Setter Property="Foreground" Value="#FF1493"/>           <!-- Deep Pink -->
        <Setter Property="FontFamily" Value="Consolas"/>
        <Setter Property="FontWeight" Value="Bold"/>
        <Setter Property="FontSize" Value="12"/>
        <Setter Property="Padding" Value="10,8"/>
        <Setter Property="BorderBrush" Value="#FF1493"/>          <!-- Pink border -->
        <Setter Property="BorderThickness" Value="0,0,1,2"/>      <!-- Right & bottom borders -->
        <Setter Property="HorizontalContentAlignment" Value="Left"/>
    </Style>
</DataGrid.ColumnHeaderStyle>
```

## ?? **THEME RATIONALE**

### **Pink Headers (#FF1493 - Deep Pink)**
- High contrast against black background
- Stands out from green grid text
- Professional yet vibrant
- Easy to read and identify

### **Black Background (#000000)**
- Matches Matrix dark theme
- Reduces eye strain
- Makes pink text pop
- Consistent with app design

### **Green Text (#00FF00)**
- Maintains Matrix theme
- Distinguishes data from headers
- High readability
- Iconic Matrix look
